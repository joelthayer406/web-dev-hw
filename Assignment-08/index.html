How to Compose a Tweet on a Computer

Step 1

![step 1](./images/step1.png)

Go to the top right corner of the monitor and click the button that says 'Tweet.'

Step 2

![step 1](./images/step2.png)

Step 3

![step 1](./images/step3.png)
